# Personal OS - Workout & Fitness System Specification V1

## Purpose

The Fitness System exists to track:

* Workout consistency
* Workout completion
* Training history
* Physical progress

This is NOT a bodybuilding app.

This is NOT a workout planner marketplace.

This is NOT a calorie calculator.

The focus is:

* Did I train?
* What did I train?
* Am I consistent?
* Am I progressing?

# Automation Rules

When workout created:

Created At = Current Date

When edited:

Updated At = Current Date

When workout completed:

Completion status updated

Analytics recalculated

When strength entry added:

Personal records recalculated

Trend charts updated

---

# Version 1 Scope

Must Include

Workout Templates

Workout Logs

Workout Checklists

Strength Tracking

Personal Records

Consistency Analytics

Workout Categories

Fitness Dashboard

Automatic Dates

---

Do Not Include

Exercise Video Library

Workout Marketplace

Social Features

Trainer Accounts

Calories Burned Estimates

AI Workout Recommendations

Wearable Device Integration

Gym Check-In Features

Complex Exercise Databases

Subscription Features

The fitness system should remain simple, fast, and focused on consistency and progress rather than becoming a full fitness platform.
