# Personal OS - Analytics System Specification V1

## Purpose

The Analytics System transforms raw tracking data into useful insights.

The purpose is not to create dozens of charts.

The purpose is to answer:

* Am I improving?
* Am I consistent?
* Where is my time going?
* What am I completing?
* What am I neglecting?

Analytics should focus on trends, consistency, and progress.

---

Analytics
│
├── Overview
├── Skills

├── Fitness

├── Goals
└── Productivity

---

# Analytics Principles

1. Trends over snapshots
2. Consistency over perfection
3. Long-term visibility
4. Minimal chart clutter
5. Actionable insights

# Life Areas

## Purpose

Life Areas are the highest-level categorization system in Personal OS.

Every major object can belong to a Life Area.

This allows the system to understand where time, effort, and progress are being invested.

Life Areas are used throughout:

* Skills
* Projects
* Goals
* Learning Sessions
* Health Goals
* Fitness Goals

and especially within Analytics.

---

## Default Life Areas

Career

Learning

Health

Fitness

Finance

Personal

Relationships

Creative

Custom
