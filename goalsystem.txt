# Personal OS - Goal System Specification V1

## Purpose

The Goal System is the highest level of the application.

Goals answer:

"Why am I doing all of this?"

Without goals:

* Skills are isolated
* Projects are isolated
* Fitness is isolated
* Health is isolated

Goals connect everything together.

---

# Progress Calculation

Goal progress should support multiple modes.

---

Mode 1

Checklist Based

Completed Checklist Items

÷

Total Checklist Items

---

Mode 2

Linked Items Based

Completed Skills

Completed Projects

Completed Goal Tasks

÷

Total Linked Items

---

Mode 3

Manual

User manually sets progress.

---

Default

Checklist Based
