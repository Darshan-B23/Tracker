# Personal OS - Health & Nutrition System Specification V1

## Purpose

The Health System exists to track:

* Nutrition
* Body composition
* Weight trends
* Health habits
* Long-term physical progress

This is NOT:

* A calorie calculator
* A diet planner
* A meal delivery system
* A medical platform

The focus is:

* What am I eating?
* Am I hitting my nutrition goals?
* Is my body moving in the right direction?
* Am I staying consistent?
